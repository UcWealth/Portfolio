About

My name is Uche Ifunanya Okeke (a.k.a. Uc Wealth) and I live in Lagos, Nigeria.
I am a Full Stack Developer who enjoys creating beautiful, functional and scalable web applications using tools such as Node and Express, React,CSS, Bootstrap, and MongoDB.

I am quite passionate about Technology and Programming, and how they can be used to improve the living standard of Africans. I've always enjoyed building things, acquiring relevant knowledge and contributing to Social Development and Educational Initiatives.
